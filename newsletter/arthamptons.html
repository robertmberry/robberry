Leo Jensen

"Sighs on the Midway", Oil on wood, photomontage, toy gun, and mixed media, 29 x 27 x 7", 1965

Robert Berry will be working with skilled and masterful contemporary artists promoting their work, and placing them in exhibitions, private, public, and corporate collections. My areas of specialty encompass modern and post-war American art, abstract painting and sculpture by artists at mid-century, and contemporary painting. I also have works by Ron Gorchov and Takesada Matsutani available by request.


I will offer a variety of services to clients interested in purchasing quality works of art including:

  • Identifying and executing a collecting or single-purchase buying strategy
  • Sourcing works of art at auction, galleries and from artists and dealers
  • Researching and analyzing market price and long-term value of each piece
  • Recommending purchases of artwork appropriate to each collector
  • Coordinating purchases and completing transactions on behalf of clients

    • I will be at ArtHamptons with Rediscovered Masters from July 10-13th, 2014. Leo Jensen, Harry Bertschmann, Geoffrey Moss, Eugene Healy, Qasim Sabti, and Don ZanFagna will all be on display.


    For information on the artists and available work, or if you would like to request a pass to ArtHamptons, please contact me.
